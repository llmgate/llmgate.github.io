LLM GATE

What's this?

I spent over 3 years at Snap Inc. building a gateway service for 3D Bitmojis. The goal of the service was to apply a lot of practicies to save money, improve latency, and ensure high availability while handling 5PB+ of data and serving billions of daily requests.

Now I want to build a scalable gateway for your LLMs so you don't have to worry about any of these.
